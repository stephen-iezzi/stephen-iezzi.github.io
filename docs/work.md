## My Background

Previously, I’ve worked on a variety of machine learning and LLM projects including a computer vision system for a Fortune 500 client and an LLM-powered chatbot for a leading publisher built on top of a large content archive.

While my background is broader than LLM workflows alone, the area I’ve chosen to focus on is turning unstructured documents and conversations into structured outputs that can be used reliably inside real business workflows.
